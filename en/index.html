About Us

Kahveci Law Office was established by Vehbi Kahveci in 1983. The firm purposes to provide an excellent service to local or foreign companies and individuals, with its professional crew since 1983. The attorneys at the Kahveci Law Office are capable of speaking at least a foreign language at an advanced degree and providing attorney and legal consulting services to the clients in English and Turkish languages. Beside the attorneys at the office, Kahveci Law Office works in collaboration with the law firms that established by home-grown attorneys. Kahveci Law Office also gets support from expert academists and lawyers on Commercial Law, Corporate Law and Contracts areas to provide better service to their clients. Kahveci Law Office represents their clients at any administrative and judicial authority, and helping and consulting them in all necessary issues by sticking to councel keeping and keeping their clients’ benefits above everything.
